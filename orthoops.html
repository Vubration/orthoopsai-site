What Gets Installed

14 Core Systems.
Fully Configured. Day One.

This isn't a software license. It's a complete operational infrastructure, installed and configured on your hardware, loaded with your data.

◆
OpenClaw Gateway The always-on AI backbone. Runs silently in the background, routes every request, keeps the whole system connected and alive around the clock.
◆
Telegram Group Architecture Pre-configured groups for field reps, admin ops, warehouse/logistics, and an executive channel. Structured communication, not chaos.
◆
Pricing Lookup System SQLite-cached pricing with instant response times. Handles modifier logic, hospital and customer resolution, and multi-tier contract lookups automatically.
◆
D-Ticket & PO Extraction Pipeline Reps photograph POs or upload PDFs. Gemini Flash extracts the data, validates it, and auto-files to Google Drive. Zero manual entry.
◆
Master File Automation 5-source rebuild pipeline that pulls from every data source, validates against your rep roster and account list, and delivers a clean master file on demand.
◆
Morning Board Briefing PDFs Every morning, a structured executive summary is auto-generated and delivered: open orders, rep activity, pricing flags, escalations, and priorities for the day.
◆
3-Layer Knowledge System Knowledge Vault for raw documents. Institutional Brain for reasoning patterns. Rapport Files for per-person communication style. The system gets smarter over time.
◆
Self-Healing & Monitoring 27+ automated health checks running continuously. If anything breaks, the system detects it, restarts the affected component, and alerts you — before you notice.
◆
Session Cost Control Automated resets prevent runaway API costs. The system manages its own token economy — you don't have to watch spend or manually intervene.
◆
Decision Record & Judge System Every critical operation is evidence-based and logged. The Judge evaluates decisions against defined criteria before they execute — no guessing, no errors.
◆
Roundtable Architecture Multi-group observation with consolidated summaries. The system watches all channels simultaneously and surfaces what matters — without requiring you to read everything.
◆
Escalation Matrix Structured routing for exceptions, approvals, and issues. The right person gets the right alert at the right time — not everything, just what needs a human.
◆
Web-Based PO Deep Dive Analyzer Browser-based tool for in-depth PO analysis with hospital billing intelligence. Drill into any purchase order with full context and history.
◆
Full Configuration & Onboarding We load your rep roster, pricing files, hospital accounts, and product data. We build your Telegram groups, configure your Chiefs, and hand you a running system.
