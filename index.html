Common Questions

What hardware do I need?

An Apple Mac Mini (M4 or newer recommended), 16GB RAM, 256GB+ storage. Cost: ~$599-$799. It runs 24/7 in your office connected to your network.

What are the ongoing costs?

AI processing fees (Anthropic + Google APIs) typically run under $200/month for a full operation. No monthly software subscriptions. No platform fees. No per-seat charges.

Is my data secure?

Everything runs on YOUR hardware, in YOUR office. No cloud servers. No vendor access. Your pricing, your commissions, your patient data — none of it leaves your network unless you want it to.

How long until I'm fully operational?

With the Pre-Installed option, your AI is responding within minutes of plugging it in. Full operational deployment (all agents, all pipelines) takes 6-8 weeks depending on your team's pace.

Do my reps need to learn new software?

No. They use Telegram — which works like any group chat. They text questions, send photos, drop files. The AI handles the rest. If they can use iMessage, they can use this.

What if I need help after purchase?

The Blueprint includes a complete build guide that your AI reads and follows. For hands-on help, the Full Onboarding package includes 3 months of direct support from the builder.
