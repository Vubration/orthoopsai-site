Visual Intelligence

Point a camera. The AI does the rest.

OrthoOps AI reads surgical trays, implant bins, X-rays, and inventory photos with the same intelligence as a 14-year veteran in the field.

🦴

X-Ray & Fluoroscopy

Photograph any X-ray or live C-arm image. The system identifies the implant manufacturer, model, and orientation — instantly. Reps use this in the OR, in hallway conversations with surgeons, or when evaluating revision cases.

⚠️ Image quality and angle affect accuracy. Best results with clear AP or lateral views. Works as a strong starting point — always verify with clinical judgment.

📦

Bin & Tray Verification

Every bin has a defined contents list. The warehouse team photographs a bin before it ships or after it returns — the AI cross-references every part number, confirms the bin is complete, and logs the result automatically. No manual counting. No guessing.

📋

Inventory Movement Tracking

Photograph a stack of trays being moved to a facility. The AI reads every label, logs every asset number, and updates your master inventory tracker — which rep has what, where it is, and when it moved. No spreadsheet entry. Just a photo.

How It Works in Practice
→Warehouse ships a bin — tech photographs it, AI confirms all parts are present and logs the shipment
→Rep receives inventory — photographs trays on arrival, AI reads every label and updates location tracking
→Post-case restocking — warehouse photographs returned bins, AI checks against what was sent and flags anything missing
→Annual audit — reps photograph every tray at their accounts, AI builds the complete audit record automatically
